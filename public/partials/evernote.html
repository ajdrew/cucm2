Use your 2nd Brain!

  • August 31, 2016
  • Evernote

  • Do you have good productivity at work?
  • Do you forget things?
  • Have you digitalized all your paperwork?
  • Do you have a backup of all your critical paperwork?

These questions are how Evernote can help you. There are hundreds of people who have excellent tips and tricks for getting stuff done and archiving your digital life. However, very few of these systems are exactly the way you work. That is where someone like me can give you a hand to adapt your current productivty system into more and better. You can take the great blank canvas of Evernote to build that digital archive and productivity system.

Below are my recommendations for getting setup with Evernote:

  • Hit up your local library first, instead of Amazon to save money. If the library doesn't have it, see if they will order it. By the time they get it, you will probably be ready for it.
  • Get an awesome Bluetooth headset. Suggestions: LG 700 or Plantronics M165
  • Turn off the radio and start listening to something worth while.

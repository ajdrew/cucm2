I'm usually not a setimental guy, but when it comes to cigars; I might wax eloquent.

While I consider cigars a quinticential trait of manliness, I do no think differently of those who do not partake. No judgement. I find that the time spent with a cigar is mostly likely good conversation, refreashing silence, or reflective contemplation. These are experiences that men should strive for.

My tastes, after having done this for goodness knows how long, tend toward the medium to heavier categories with hints of cedar, coffee, light spice, and a creamy filler.

Note to beginners: Take your time and enjoy the variety and process. In time, all will be revealed. O, and the Drew Estates Java with Jameson is an excellent start.

Below are my recommendations for partaking in cigars:

  • Find a relaxing place to sit either alone or with a good friend.
  • Obtain a good lighter (Colibri Firebird and good butane) or good matches.
  • An enjoyable drink (Sometimes Scotch straight, beer, plain Coke, or coffe with Kalua).

While it is difficult to not recommend more, here are my top recommendations:

Ave Maria

Knights Templar

Lionheart

Holy Grail

Reconquista *Heavily spiced and leathery.

Argentum

Being Catholic, they had me at the name. Beyond the name, the cigars have the utmost quality, flavor, and quantity of a rich smoke that puts this brand on the top of my list.

Padron

1964 *Best creamy and rich smoke.

Handmade Series

For a more full bodied smoker, this gives you just enough kick in a shorter package for an even quality smoke.

Drew Estates

Java *Best flavore infused.

Undercrown *Best medium to full body.

Java Mint

Tabak *Excellent coffee flavor.

Kuba Kuba *Fun and creamy.

From a beginner perspective, you can't go wrong with a Java. Excellent flavored wrapper which gives you something to smack your lips and an easy rich smoke on the shorter side.

Man 'O War

Virtue *Best light, rich, and creamy.

Runination *Best full bodied.

Armada

These cigars are not for the feint of heart. Do not expect to stand up after smoking one of these and rock and roll. These are full bodied and will seriously get your relax on.

Camacho

Triple Maduro *Serious power cigar

Camacho is one of the original brands to make it out of Cuba in tact. They bring the power and quality that Cuba was known for. Be forwarned, do not underestimate this cigar.

Alec Bradley

All American *Terrifically complex with lots of it.

Prensado

Tempus

Grab this cigar when you want a serious full bodied relaxation session. These are a step down from the Camancho, but come prepared to be called a smoker for the evening.
